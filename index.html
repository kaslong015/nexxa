About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Critical Minerals and Clean Technologies

At Nexxa Terra Tech Ltd, we stand at the intersection of exploration, sustainable mining, and advanced technology, driving Africa’s role in the global green energy transition. Through our two major arms Nexxa Terra and Nexxa Tech we provide end-to-end solutions from critical minerals supply to cutting-edge innovations that shape tomorrow’s industries.

Nexxa Terra Tech Ltd is a dynamic, future-focused enterprise committed to creating sustainable value in the global energy and technology landscape.

We operate through two major divisions:
  • Nexxa Terra Anchored in critical minerals, exploration, mining, and advisory, this unit secures and delivers the natural resources essential for renewable energy, electric vehicles, and advanced industries
  • Nexxa Tech Focused on software, renewable energy, robotics, and electrical systems, this unit delivers innovative technologies that accelerate efficiency, automation, and clean energy solutions.
Our vision is simple

To be Africa’s trusted partner in driving the green economy through sustainable resources and transformative technologies
